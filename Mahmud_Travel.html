Hot Water Beach, New Zealand

Imagine immersing yourself in the refreshing warm water in the most beautiful beach you've ever been to. See yourself relaxing and reclining in the shade of massive cliffs.You could have all this and more by traveling to Hot Water Beach, New Zealand . You have the unique opportunity to experience Aboriginal and Kiwi culture. Wouldn't you want to give yourself the gift of exploration?

Hot Water Beach is located on Mercury Bay on the eastern coast of the Coromandel Peninsula. It is about 8 miles south east of Whitianga, and approximately a 2-hour car ride from Auckland, the capital. There is a myriad of activities to do in the area. There are the Cathedral Cove Kayak Tours and if you enjoy hiking, there is Mount Paku.
